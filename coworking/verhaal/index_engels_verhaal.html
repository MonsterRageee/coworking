Los Pollos Hermanos is a charming family business that has its origins in the heart of a small village called Saltillo. This food paradise was founded by none other than Gus Fring, together with his devoted brother, Max Arciniega. Together they call themselves the "Chicken Brothers," a name that has become synonymous with delicious culinary experiences and a warm, family atmosphere.
